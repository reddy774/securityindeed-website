Honest about our architecture

We believe transparency builds trust. Here's exactly where your data lives:

  • SMS & email content — scanned on your phone by on-device AI. Never uploaded. No internet required.
  • Breach monitoring — your email addresses are checked against breach databases via our encrypted server.
  • Data broker removal — submitted via our partner Optery's API. We send only what's needed to submit removal requests.
  • Account data — stored encrypted on our self-hosted server. We never sell or share your information.
